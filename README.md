### The investment plan (assumptions)

- Invest **HK$40,000/month**, split **equally** into 5 ETFs (**HK$8,000 each**), for **12 months** → **HK$480,000 invested** (note: 12 × 40k = 480k, not 500k).
- **60 buy trades/year** (5 ETFs × 12 months). End-of-year holding ≈ HK$480k; average holding over the year ≈ HK$260k.
- **~39 dividend-collection events/year** (see ETF table) — each one triggers a separate dividend handling fee.
- **Year 1:** holdings are small, so 0.5% of each distribution is below the minimum fee → **every dividend is charged the bank's minimum.**
- **ETFs are exempt from HK stamp duty**, so government/exchange fees are tiny (~HK$170/year) and identical across all 5 banks.

### Key insights — why the ranking flips

1. **Small buys → "is there a minimum commission?" matters more than the rate.** Wing Lung (no min) wins; ICBC (min $88) and DBS (min $100) sink despite great rates.
2. **Monthly payers × 5 ETFs → ~39 dividend events/year, each charged the minimum.** BOCHK's HK$75 minimum is ~2.5× the others — its dividend handling alone (HK$2,925) is nearly its whole cost.
3. **BOCHK's #2 rank is a mirage.** It is propped up entirely by a $0-commission *promotion*, while being the **most expensive on the exact fee a dividend investor cares about**. If the promo ends or monthly turnover exceeds HK$500k, BOCHK becomes the worst.

**Worked example — collecting a HK$560 monthly dividend:** 0.5% = HK$2.80, below every minimum, so you pay the minimum: HK$20 (ICBC) / HK$30 (most) / **HK$75 (BOCHK)**. At BOCHK that is **13.4% of the dividend eaten by the handling fee.**

### Mainland-China tax-resident note (withholding tax ≠ handling fee)

- **Dividend collection fee** (computed above) is the **bank's handling charge** — it varies by bank.
- **Dividend withholding tax** is a **tax**, set by the underlying holding and your tax status — **identical across all 5 banks**, so it does **not** affect which bank is cheapest. It only changes your net dividend.
  - These ETFs hold many **H-shares / mainland companies** (esp. 03437 SOEs, 03416 HSCEI) → mainland dividends are withheld **~10% at the fund level** before you receive them.
  - **Pure HK companies / REITs:** Hong Kong has **no dividend withholding tax** (0%).
  - **Covered-call ETF (03416)** distributions are largely option premium / return of capital — generally not taxed as dividends.
- **Channel difference:** buying mainland H-shares via **Stock Connect (港股通)** withholds **20%** for individuals; buying HK-listed ETFs through a **HK bank** (your case) is taxed at the fund level (~10%). As a PRC tax resident you may still have self-reporting obligations on offshore income — **consult a tax professional.**

### How to cut costs further

1. **Buy quarterly, not monthly** → 60 trades drop to 20. Saves SC (~$1,000), ICBC (~$1,760), DBS (~$2,000). No effect on Wing Lung (no min) or BOCHK ($0 promo).
2. **Hold fewer monthly-paying ETFs** → fewer dividend events. 3 of your 5 pay monthly and drive ~33 of ~39 events.
3. The **0.5% rate is unavoidable**, but larger, less-frequent distributions avoid hitting the per-event minimum.

### Bottom line

For this **monthly DCA into 5 dividend ETFs**, use **CMB Wing Lung Sunflower** (≈ HK$2,180/yr; the no-minimum commission fits small DCA perfectly). **Do not use BOCHK to collect dividends** (highest dividend fee). SC Priority suits a "fewer, larger, buy-and-hold" style; ICBC and DBS are poor fits for this small, high-frequency pattern.

### 关键洞察 — 排名为何翻转

1. **小额买入 →「有没有最低佣金」比费率更重要。** 永隆（无最低）赢；工银（最低 $88）、星展（最低 $100）虽费率好却垫底。
2. **月派息 × 5 只 → 一年约 39 次派息，每次按最低扣。** 中银最低 $75，约为别家 2.5 倍——光股息费一项（$2,925）几乎就是它的全部成本。
3. **中银的第 2 名是「纸面」的。** 完全靠 $0 佣金**促销**撑着，却在分红投资者最在意的费用上**全场最贵**。促销一结束、或月周转超 50 万，中银立刻变最贵。

**实例 — 收一笔 HK$560 的月股息：** 0.5% = HK$2.80，低于所有最低收费，故按最低扣：HK$20（工银）/ HK$30（多数）/ **HK$75（中银）**。在中银，这笔手续费**吃掉股息的 13.4%**。

### 大陆税务居民提示（预扣税 ≠ 手续费）

- **代收股息费**（上面算的）= **银行手续费**，各家不同。
- **股息预扣税** = **税**，由底层持仓与你的税务身份决定，**5 家完全一样**，因此**不影响选哪家最便宜**，只影响你到手净额。
  - 这些 ETF 大量持有 **H 股/内地企业**（尤其 03437 央企、03416 国企）→ 内地派息在**基金层面预扣约 10%**后才到你手。
  - **纯香港公司/REITs：** 香港**不征股息预扣税**（0%）。
  - **覆盖式认购期权 ETF（03416）** 的分派大多是期权金/资本返还，一般不按股息预扣。
- **通道差异：** 走内地**港股通**买 H 股，个人预扣 **20%**；用**香港银行**买香港上市 ETF（你的情况）按基金层面（约 10%）。作为内地税务居民，境外收益或有自行申报义务——**请咨询专业税务师。**

### 如何把成本再压低

1. **改每季买、别每月买** → 60 笔降到 20 笔。渣打省约 $1,000、工银约 $1,760、星展约 $2,000。对永隆（无最低）和中银（$0 推广）无影响。
2. **少几只月月派息 ETF** → 减少派息次数。你 5 只里 3 只月派息，贡献了约 39 次里的约 33 次。
3. **0.5% 费率无法避免**，但单次派息越大、次数越少，越不容易被最低收费惩罚。

### 一句话建议

这套「**每月定投 5 只分红 ETF**」用 **招商永隆金葵花** 最省（≈ HK$2,180/年；无最低佣金特别契合小额定投）。**别用中银香港收息**（股息费最贵）。渣打优先适合「少次大额 + 长揸」；工银、星展在这套小额高频打法下不划算。
